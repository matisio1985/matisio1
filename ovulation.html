Ovulation Calculator

Enter your last period date and cycle length to estimate your next ovulation and fertile days.

What is Ovulation?

Ovulation is the release of an egg from your ovary. It's the most fertile time of your menstrual cycle, and typically occurs about 14 days before your next period starts.

Why track ovulation?

Tracking ovulation helps improve your chances of getting pregnant. Knowing your fertile window can guide you in planning or preventing pregnancy naturally.

How does this calculator work?

By entering the first day of your last menstrual period and your average cycle length, this tool predicts your ovulation day and the days you're most likely to conceive.
