What is VAT?

VAT stands for Value Added Tax and is a type of consumption tax used in many countries. It's added to goods and services at each stage of production or distribution.

How does this calculator help?

You can add VAT to a net price to get the gross amount, or remove VAT from a gross amount to find the base price. Perfect for shopping, invoicing, or freelance work.
