About This Tool

This ovulation calculator estimates your most fertile days based on the first day of your last menstrual period and your average cycle length. It assumes ovulation occurs 14 days before your next period.

Why Track Ovulation?

Understanding your fertile window can improve your chances of pregnancy or help avoid it. Timing is everything when planning conception.

Fertile Window Explained
