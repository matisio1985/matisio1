What is the Pomodoro Technique?

The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo. It breaks work into 25-minute focused sessions followed by 5-minute breaks to help maintain productivity and avoid burnout.

How to use this timer

Press "Start" to begin a 25-minute focus session. When time is up, take a 5-minute break. Repeat this cycle four times, then take a longer break.

Why use a Pomodoro timer?

It helps reduce distractions, improve concentration, and boost motivation. Perfect for studying, remote work, or any deep focus task.
