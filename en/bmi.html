Understanding Your BMI Result

Your Body Mass Index is a quick screening tool, not a diagnostic test. It estimates body fat based on your height and weight. A high BMI can signal higher risks for conditions such as type¬†2 diabetes, heart disease and sleep apnea. A very low BMI may indicate nutrient deficiencies or other health issues.
