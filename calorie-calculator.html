What is a Calorie Calculator?

A calorie calculator helps you estimate how many calories your body needs each day based on your age, gender, height, weight, and physical activity level. It's a useful tool for managing weight, building muscle, or improving your overall health.

How does it work?

The calculator uses the Mifflin-St Jeor formula to estimate your Basal Metabolic Rate (BMR), which is then adjusted for your activity level. This provides an estimate of your Total Daily Energy Expenditure (TDEE), or how many calories you burn per day.

Why track your calorie needs?

Understanding your daily calorie requirement can help you reach your fitness goals faster. Whether you're looking to lose weight, gain muscle, or maintain your current physique, having a daily calorie target is essential.
